About

JFKHS Robotics Club | VEX Team #8941

The JFKHS Robotics Club is located at John F. Kennedy High School, Guam, USA.

We have a competitive team that goes to an annual VEX Worlds Competition with the goal of creating a robot designed for a game and have it compete with other teams.

Our long-term goal is to create a machine dedicated to survey the depths of our island's oceans and provide us information for research.
