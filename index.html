JFKHS ROBOTICS

Robotics Honors Class

JFKHS provides all students with exciting opportunities to engage in STEM-related learning by participating in a well-established robotics program. This year marks the beginning of the sixth year of robotics at JFKHS providing students both a club and a senior-level course to engage all levels in the fun and excitement of robotics.

Robotics Club

The school has represented Guam the past two years at the VEX Robotics World Championships, and the JFKHS VEX team looks forward to an exciting season this year.
